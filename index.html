Why PetSmile

Every year, wonderful pets and loving families miss each other simply because they never meet.

PetSmile brings shelters and adopters together in one place, powered by AI that removes the busywork on both sides.

Shelters go from a few photos and notes to a complete, ready-to-publish pet profile in minutes, not hours.

Adopters swipe through pets who are already a real match for their home, so no one wastes time on a good-bye that was never going to work.

Every match opens a live video chat — and when that goes well, an in-person visit, turning a swipe into a new best friend.
